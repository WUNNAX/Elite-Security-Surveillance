Elite Solutions is the number one choice in the management of integrated automation systems, thanks to a complete variety of smart and user-friendly products.

An Elite Solutions story

The cultures, languages and heritage of South Africa are diverse and dynamic. The ideology of Elite Solutions is to create relevant solutions for our customers, blending the best global technology with a detailed understanding of the local environment. In support of our ideology, Elite Solutions identified and invested in a proudly South African company in 2015 - ET Systems, who had a proven track record as leaders in a very competitive industry. As designing our world-class products for South Africa & Africa was an Elite Solutions priority, we continued to intensely research the unique needs of local installers and consumers, the challenges of the environment and the expectations of the market. Today, the outcome of this process is proudly presented in the most comprehensive and extensive Elite Solutions offering, directly created for markets on the African continent and beyond.
